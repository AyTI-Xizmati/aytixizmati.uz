## 5 main ERP implementation mistakes

1. **Launching everything at once** — biggest mistake. Phase it (1 module = 1 month).
2. **Not training staff** — best ERP is useless without trained users.
3. **Abrupt cutover** — need 1–2 months of parallel operation.
4. **IT-only decision** — every department must participate.
5. **Not calculating ROI** — calculate savings BEFORE implementation.
