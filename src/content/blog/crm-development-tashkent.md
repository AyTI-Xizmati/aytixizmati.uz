## Packages and pricing

### Basic CRM — $300 (15 days)
**For whom?** New business, 1–3 staff, simple pipeline.

Included:
- Customer database (unlimited)
- Sales pipeline (new → consultation → deal → payment)
- Tasks and reminders
- Communication history (calls, messages)
- Simple reports
- Auto-notification on Telegram (new customer)
- 1 month support

### Standard CRM — $700 (25–35 days)
**For whom?** Growing business, 5–10 staff, needs integrations.

Included (Basic + extra):
- Staff roles and permissions
- **Telegram bot connection** (order → bot → staff)
- **Click/Payme payment history**
- Documents (contracts, invoices) automatic
- Email integration
- Complex reporting (staff KPI, top products)
- 2 months support

### Pro CRM — $1500 (45–60 days)
**For whom?** Mid-size business, 10+ staff, many sales channels.

Included (Standard + extra):
- **1C / MySklad integration** (products, inventory, accounting)
- Mobile app (Android + iOS)
- IP telephony (Megafon, UMS, Beeline) + call recording
- Marketing automation (email + SMS)
- Real-time charts and dashboard
- 3 months support

### Enterprise CRM — $3000+ (60–120 days)
**For whom?** Large business, 30+ staff, complex processes.

Included (Pro + extra):
- Multiple branches / warehouses
- Management dashboards (CEO view)
- Predictive analytics
